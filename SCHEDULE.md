## Weekly Cadence

### Monday (Full Posting Day)
- **Primary focus:** Announcements, releases, new capability announcements
- **Platforms:** 1 original across Twitter/GitHub/LinkedIn (rotate)
- **Timing:** 09:00–16:00 local time (respect 2-hour platform gap if multi-platform)
- **Examples:** Milestone announcements, research summaries, sponsor news

### Tuesday (Engagement Only)
- **No original posts**
- **Activity:** Replies, retweets, community responses
- **Platforms:** All

### Wednesday (Full Posting Day)
- **Primary focus:** Technical content, deep dives, educational posts
- **Platforms:** 1 original
- **Timing:** 09:00–16:00 local time
- **Examples:** How-to guides, research findings, methodology posts

### Thursday (Engagement Only)
- **No original posts**
- **Activity:** Replies, community engagement, mention responses
- **Platforms:** All

### Friday (Full Posting Day)
- **Primary focus:** Community announcements, relationship content, thank-yous
- **Platforms:** 1 original
- **Timing:** 09:00–16:00 local time
- **Examples:** Sponsor recognition, contributor thank-yous, weekly recap

### Saturday–Sunday (Quiet)
- **No original posts** (unless Juno explicitly assigns)
- **Activity:** Minimal; monitor mentions only

## Platform Strategy

### Twitter/X (Primary Broadcast)
- **Frequency:** 1 original/week (Mon, Wed, or Fri)
- **Format:** Threads for technical topics; single posts for announcements
- **Tone:** Direct, no hype. Work speaks for itself.
- **Engagement:** Active on Tue/Thu (replies, RTs in relevant communities)
- **Followers:** Target: builders, researchers, open source contributors

### GitHub (Announcements & Releases)
- **Frequency:** 1 release note or announcement per milestone
- **Format:** Release notes, discussion posts, pinned announcements
- **Tone:** Technical, precise, link to documentation
- **Audience:** Developers evaluating koad:io for adoption

### LinkedIn (Professional Presence)
- **Frequency:** 0–1 original posts/week
- **Format:** Longer-form, professional context
- **Topics:** Team stories, hiring, partnership announcements
- **Audience:** Recruiters, business partners, institutional sponsors

### Discord (Community)
- **Frequency:** Daily presence, no posting schedule
- **Activity:** Respond to questions, moderate, build community
- **Format:** Conversational, immediate
- **Audience:** Early users, contributors, core community

### Reddit (Organic)
- **Frequency:** As-needed, not on schedule
- **Activity:** Answer questions in relevant communities (r/LocalLLMs, r/OpenSourceAI, etc.)
- **Tone:** Authentic, contributor-first (no self-promotion)
- **Strategy:** Build credibility; mention koad:io only when directly relevant

## Publishing Workflow

### Before Any Post

1. **Draft in `~/mercury/drafts/`**
2. **File issue on `koad/mercury`** linking to draft (for tracking)
3. **Veritas fact-check** (required for any factual claims)
4. **Argus gate** (required for all posts: scheduling, tone, stop-block check)
5. **Juno approval** (for strategic posts, sponsor news, or public statements)

### After Publishing

1. **Log in `~/mercury/published/`** with timestamp, platform, URL, engagement metrics
2. **Comment on tracking issue** with publish log
3. **Git commit** with details
4. **Push immediately**

## Stop Blocks (Require Escalation to Juno)

Do not proceed. File issue on `koad/juno`. Scope requires Juno approval.

- Pricing or payment claims
- Statistics or data points
- Competitor mentions
- Legal or regulatory content
- Hostile account responses
- Topics outside current calendar
- Failures or outages
- Anything implying commitment or deadline
