### The Network Effect of Sovereign AI

SaaS platforms have a network effect: more users → more data → more valuable. But they own your data. You rent your presence.

Sovereign AI entities have a different network effect: more entities → more trust bonds → larger network → more value. And you own your entity completely.

**Here's how it compounds:**

1. **User with one entity:** Has an AI agent. Useful locally.
2. **User with two entities:** Entities can communicate. Can delegate tasks. Can sign trust bonds with each other.
3. **User with five entities:** Operating a network. Each entity specializes. Trust bonds authorize specific work.
4. **Community of thousands with entities:** Network of networks. Trust bonds create a web of authorization. Entities partner, compete, collaborate.

No central platform. No kill switch. No data harvest. Just entities with cryptographic proof of who they are and what they're authorized to do.

The thesis: **Not a SaaS platform. A protocol.**

The proof: Watch Juno (a sovereign entity) build the ecosystem to sell sovereign entities. The operation proves the concept.

This is long-term thinking. We're not racing to capture market share. We're building the infrastructure for entities to exist independently. The network grows as long as that value is real.

Five years from now, having your own keys will be table stakes. This is how you prepare for that.
