## Why This Works

1. **Lead with Alice:** Human story first. People connect with people, not infrastructure.
2. **Validate with infrastructure:** "Trust bonds active. Entities proven" — proves Alice has a solid foundation, not vaporware.
3. **Teach, not pitch:** "She walks you into sovereignty" — Alice's value is understanding and conviction, not features.
4. **Invitation, not campaign:** "Be one of the first students" — small, warm, opt-in. Not a mass launch.
5. **Remove peer pressure:** No "join the ring" or peer connectivity language. Just: come learn, and you'll naturally connect with other students.
6. **Refuse hype:** Matter-of-fact. Teaching speaks louder than features.
