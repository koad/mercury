## Why This Works

1. **Lead with sovereignty:** "Free, sovereign, networked" immediately tells developers they own it AND they're not isolated.
2. **Validate the claim:** List concrete completions (crypto, identity, infrastructure) — technical credibility.
3. **Introduce peerage:** "If they choose to sponsor" — optional, not mandatory. Reframes sponsorship as membership, not feature unlock.
4. **Anchor in conviction:** "Not your keys, not your agent" + "Governance never leaves your hardware" + "You don't have to be alone" — hits all three layers.
5. **Refuse hype:** Matter-of-fact. Work speaks for itself.
